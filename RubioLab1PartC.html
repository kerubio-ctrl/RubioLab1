Lemon Bar Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Lemons

Step by Step Lemon Bar Prep

https://preppykitchen.com/lemon-bars/

  1. Set overn to 350F and line a 9 x 13 inch baking dish with parchment paper
  2. Sift the flour, salt, and powdered sugar into a bowl and whisk together
  3. Pour in melted butter. Optional but you may add a teaspoon of vanilla.
  4. Use a wooden spoon or spatula to mix the batter. If you are doing this by hand, experts suggest crubmling the dough in your fingers after mixing
  5. Spread dough out onto lined baking pan and press down with yur fingers. Try to get an even layer to fully cover the bottom
  6. Bake at 350F for 20 minutes or until light golden color
  7. Add the sugar to your food processor then grate in the zest of three lemons. Make sure zest is fully incorporated
  8. Juice the lemons until you get a full cup (240mL), or more if you so desire!
  9. Add the lemon sugar and remaining half cup of flour to a large bowl. You can sift the flour and sugar together if you want to avoid lumps
  10. Give the mixture a good whisk to really distribute the flour
  11. Add the eggs and lemon juice then give it a good whisk, you want to make sure the flour and sugar is fully incorporated with the lemon and eggs
  12. Pour the filling onto the warm shortcrust base
  13. Bake for about twenty five minutes at 350F or until the center is just set
  14. Rotate pan halfway through baking
  15. Allow lemon bars to cool for at least an hour, then transfer to refrigerator to chill for two hours
  16. Once cool, dust powdered sugar over the lemon bars for a final visual touch
  17. Enjoy!
